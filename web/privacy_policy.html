Privacy Policy for Mini Genius

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Mini Genius ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed specifically for children. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. All data processed during gameplay is stored locally on the device or handled anonymously through service providers.

2. Compliance with Regulations

We take child safety seriously and comply with the Following:

  • COPPA: Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule.
  • GDPR-K: General Data Protection Regulation for children.
  • Google Play Families Policy: Our app is designed to meet the strict requirements of the Google Play Families program.

3. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. The app may track game progress (levels unlocked, stars earned, etc.), but this data is stored strictly on the user's device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. To protect children:

  • All ad requests are flagged as child-directed (TFCD).
  • We only show ads from certified ad networks that comply with Google's families policies.
  • We do not use behavioral or personalized advertising for children.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use services like Firebase for crash reporting and basic analytics. These services are configured to handle data anonymously and in compliance with child privacy laws.

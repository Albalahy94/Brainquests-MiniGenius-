Privacy Policy for Mini Genius

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Mini Genius ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

2. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. All data processed is stored locally on the device or handled anonymously through third-party services like AdMob (configured for child-directed treatment).

3. Information Collection

We do not collect personal information such as names, addresses, or email addresses. We may collect non-personal information such as game progress, achievements, and settings, which are stored locally on your device.

4. Data Deletion and Account Removal

Because Mini Genius does not use a central server to store your personal accounts, there is no online account to delete. However, you can delete all your local data at any time.

To request data deletion:

  • Open the Mini Genius app.
  • Go to the Settings menu.
  • Scroll down to the Legal section and tap "Delete Account Data".
  • Confirm the prompt. This will permanently wipe all local progress, coins, and settings from your device.

5. Advertisements

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. These ads are configured to be "child-directed" to ensure they are appropriate for our audience and comply with COPPA and GPDR-K regulations. The ads displayed do not use personalized tracking or behavioral targeting.
